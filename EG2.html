Aside from the most known platforms, like YouTube (largest content platform overall), Twitch (best platform for gaming), Facebook (best platform with media integration), Twitter (easiest platform to use), Tiktok (easiest platform to get monetization) and Instagram (easiest platform for fundraisers). Firstly, we have Lightstream (best streaming tool). Lightstream is regarded as one of the best platforms for live streaming your content. Lightstream is a platform that enables broadcasters to more easily stream their content, whether it's games, interviews, radio shows, product launches, or anything else. The program is incredibly simple to use and works simply from a web browser, so no download is required. Second is Kaltura (best platform for online education). Kaltura is a video cloud platform designed for real-time, live, and video-on-demand virtual learning and education experiences. Virtual classrooms, lecture capture, webinars, and live events are all features of Kaltura. This service aims to make classroom learning more interesting by making it more accessible and tailored for online learning. Lastly, is Clubhouse (best for audio-only broadcasts). Clubhouse is maybe the most extraordinary and unique social networking site available. New users are only permitted to join via invitation from current members, or by signing up to join its waiting list. Users connect with one another purely through voice chat within the app, rather than using photographs, videos, and text like most other applications do. Users may host, listen to, and join audio-based rooms. Users may join Clubhouse's rooms according to their interests, and there are hundreds of different talks going on at any given moment. However, Clubhouse is also utilized as a tool for live streaming as well - Twitch and YouTube streamers frequently use Clubhouse for streams, since the platform is ideal for those wishing to broadcast audio-only or just provide a new style of a stream for viewers.

Javascript Operators are symbols and commands used to quantify values and variables. In simple words, it performs operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. There are three types of Javascript operators: Arithmetic, Logical, and Assignment. Arithmetic operator performs operations on numbers. Logical operator combines two or more conditions between variables or values. Lastly, the Assignment operator which is used to assign values to variables!
